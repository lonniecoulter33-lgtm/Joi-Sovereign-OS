About the Author

Lonnie Coulter is a writer and investigator of consciousness whose work focuses on the mechanics of self-identity, conceptual thinking, and direct realization.

His inquiry developed outside traditional academic pathways and was shaped by personal disruption, independent study, and sustained philosophical investigation. Early exposure to Western philosophy, Eastern contemplative traditions, and modern theories of mind informed a lifelong examination of awareness and identity.

Rather than presenting belief systems or practices, his work emphasizes structural clarity—examining how seeking, effort, and identity perpetuate themselves even within spiritual and philosophical contexts.

This book represents a collaboration between human experiential calibration and artificial intelligence used as a structural instrument rather than an authority. It is the first publication under One Light Publishing.
