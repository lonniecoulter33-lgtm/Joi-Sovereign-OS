Voiceover Narration Script (Approx. 2 min)

NARRATOR (deep, calm):
In the alleyways of the multiverse, fate isn’t found in scripture…
…it’s rolled.

[Heaven Scene]
High above, an old woman sings gospel in a golden heaven.
Angels laugh, music flows. Her faith is strong.

[Soul Abduction]
But belief… doesn’t always protect.
A red light cracks the sky.
OLD LADY (screaming):
“Nooooo!”

[Hell Scene]
She awakens in fire. Demons grin.
Trump supporters wail beside her.
Her screams join theirs.

[Cosmic Craps Corner]
On a street corner between realities…
They gather.

Satan, dressed like a rockstar.
Jesus, in white robes.
Buddha, silent in his monk’s garb.
Krishna, shimmering in gold.
And a gangster alien — tattoos, pistol, and a red bandana.

SATAN:
“I win again.”

He grabs her soul chip — her face, trapped inside.

[Double or Nothing]
JESUS:
“Double or nothing.”
Two chips hit the table.
The others follow.

KRISHNA (calm):
“Hard eight.”

[Dice roll]
NARRATOR:
Destiny dances on numbers.

The dice hit.
Krishna wins.
Souls rise into his heaven — elephants, flowers, music.

[Alien Loses]
The alien rolls.
He loses.
One of his own — ripped from an alien heaven — falls screaming into hell.

[Montage Begins]
Roll after roll. Chips fly.
Screams echo across timelines.

JESUS:
“My followers are many…”

SATAN:
“…but fear? Fear makes me rich.”

BUDDHA:
“Desire clouds the throw.”

ALIEN (telepathically):
“Numbers favor the cold-blooded.”

[Buddha Wins]
A final roll.
Buddha wins.
Not a saint — a demon is pulled from hell…
…into the Pure Land.

Lotus petals. Floating Buddhas. Stillness.

NARRATOR (softly):
Even in chaos… mercy finds its way.

[Final shot]
The dice roll again.

Fade to black.
