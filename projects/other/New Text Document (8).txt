COSMIC DICE — FINAL CUT

[0:00–0:15]

In the highest reaches of eternity,
where sorrow fades and memory rests,
a gentle soul sings among angels.

A life completed.
A journey fulfilled.

For her… heaven is peace.

But somewhere beyond this light…

fate is being rolled.

[0:15–0:25]

At the edge of the multiverse,
four eternal beings gather.

Not for war.

For a game.

A game of dice.

A game of souls.

The dice fall.

Wrong.

Satan laughs.

“You shouldn’t gamble with souls you can’t afford.”

Jesus lowers his head.

[0:25–0:40]

Back in heaven…

light shatters.

Fire pours through perfection.

Angels scream.

She is pulled through flame…

and dropped into hell.

[0:40–0:50]

“Double or nothing,” Satan says.

“You’re already down thousands.”

The dice roll.

Two more souls vanish.

[0:50–1:05]

A couple walks in paradise.

Laughing.

Unaware.

The sky opens.

They are taken.

[1:05–1:15]

“You’re worse than your father,” Satan says.

“He lost millions.”

Buddha laughs.

Krishna claps.

Jesus sinks lower.

[1:15–1:20]

Now they kneel in ash.

Beside the old woman.

Learning too late.

[1:20–1:30]

Krishna rolls.

Perfect.

A soul leaves Jesus.

Laughter echoes.

[1:30–1:45]

A man walks beside God.

Krishna takes his hand.

Pulls him away.

He finds paradise.

[1:45–2:00]

Buddha faces Satan.

The dice fall.

A soul is freed.

[2:00–2:10]

In hell, a golden portal opens.

Buddha lifts a forgotten soul.

Upward.

[2:10–2:20]

In the Pure Land,
even demons find peace.

[2:20–2:30]

Alone, Jesus kneels.

“Father… help me.”

“I’ve lost too much.”

[2:30–2:35]

The sky splits.

God descends.

[2:35–2:45]

God joins the game.

Wine flows.

Laughter rises.

Jesus holds the souls.

[2:45–2:50]

The dice fall.

Wrong.

Again.

[2:50–3:00]

Girls dance in heaven.

Unaware…

their souls are wagered.

[3:00–3:10]

Jesus hands them over.

God erupts.

Lightning burns his eyes.

Buddha and Krishna collapse laughing.

[3:10–3:25]

Fire opens.

Angels scream.

Girls fall into hell.

No answers.

Only loss.

[3:25–3:55]

Back at the table…

the game continues.

God speaks softly.

“Sometimes you lose.”

“Sometimes you win.”

“You can’t win…

if you don’t play.”

The dice roll again.

And somewhere…

in every universe…

the cosmic game…

continues.
