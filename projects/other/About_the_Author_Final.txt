About the Author

Lonnie Coulter is an independent philosophical writer whose work explores consciousness, identity, and the subtle mechanisms through which seeking perpetuates itself.

Rather than presenting doctrine or practice, his writing focuses on structural clarity—removing assumptions, positions, and interpretive habits that quietly sustain the sense of separation.

The Actor Takes a Bow is his first published work and forms the foundation for future writing concerned with lived realization and the reintegration of insight into ordinary life.
