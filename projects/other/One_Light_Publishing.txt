One Light Publishing

One Light Publishing is an independent imprint dedicated to works that investigate consciousness, identity, and human understanding without reliance on doctrine, authority, or prescribed belief.

The imprint supports writing that prioritizes inquiry over instruction, clarity over consolation, and structural honesty over persuasion.

Publications under One Light Publishing explore the intersection of philosophy, contemplative insight, lived experience, and emerging technologies—including artificial intelligence—as tools for examination rather than explanation.

One Light Publishing does not promote systems of attainment or ideological positions. Its purpose is to create space for precise inquiry into what remains when conceptual frameworks fall away.
