🎥 Runway Gen-2 Full Prompt (2-Minute Multiverse Soul Game)

Paste the entire prompt into the multi-shot editor — each line/block is a new shot with cue, action, time, and transition baked in.

📽️ Shot 1: Heaven Opening (0–10 sec)

Wide shot – golden heaven with glowing clouds. Angels in white robes float around smiling. A sweet old lady in a pastel church dress claps joyfully and sings gospel. Light beams radiate. Soft heavenly harp music plays.
🎥 Camera slowly pans from clouds down to her smiling face.

📽️ Shot 2: Soul Abduction (10–15 sec)

Sudden red beam shoots from sky. Close-up on the old lady’s shocked face.
She screams “Nooooo!” as she’s yanked upward violently. Angels panic, one drops a harp.
🎥 Quick zoom-in on her face, then cut to black with a soul-screech SFX.

📽️ Shot 3: Hell Landing (15–25 sec)

Flames erupt. The old lady crashes down into a chaotic fiery hellscape. Screaming souls writhe in lava.
Several wear MAGA hats and Trump shirts, burning in torment.
Demons laugh. Fire crackles.
🎥 Slow pan as camera rises from fire to show the chaos.

📽️ Shot 4: Cosmic Craps Table Reveal (25–35 sec)

Zoom out from Hell to a glowing neon alley floating in space — galaxies spiral behind it.
Five iconic figures stand at a glowing craps table:

Jesus in white robe

Satan dressed like Keith Richards, cigarette in hand

Buddha in saffron robe, calm

Krishna adorned in gold and jewels

Gangster Alien: gray skin, red bandana, pistol, gang tattoos

Satan grins and says, “I win again!”

🎥 Dramatic pull-in shot on the table. Dice clack.

📽️ Shot 5: Soul Chip Close-Up (35–40 sec)

Close-up of Satan lifting a translucent soul-chip with the old lady’s screaming face inside.
Jesus frowns.
Krishna, Buddha, and the alien laugh quietly.
🎥 Push-in close shot on the glowing chip. Whispered “Nooo...” from inside chip.

📽️ Shot 6: Double or Nothing (40–50 sec)

Jesus steps forward.
He places two soul chips on the table.
“Double or nothing,” he says.
Others nod. Everyone throws a soul-chip in.
🎥 Camera tracks across each face as they place bets.

📽️ Shot 7: Krishna Calls It (50–60 sec)

Krishna smiles.
“Hard eight,” he says.
Dice roll dramatically.
🎥 Slow motion roll, camera low and rotating around dice midair.

📽️ Shot 8: Krishna Wins (60–70 sec)

Dice stop on hard eight.
Krishna laughs and scoops the chips.
Above him, a Hindu paradise materializes:
Golden temples, elephants, flower petals falling, dancing gopi girls welcome 8 glowing souls.
🎥 Transition to top-down shot of souls rising into the sky.

📽️ Shot 9: Alien Loses (70–80 sec)

Alien rolls the dice, confident.
Satan smirks — he wins again.
In a separate cutaway, an alien soul in robes floats peacefully, then suddenly gets sucked down screaming into hell, landing next to the old lady.
🎥 Quick jump cut between dice roll → alien heaven → fiery descent.

📽️ Shot 10: Montage – Soul Rolls (80–100 sec)

Fast-paced montage of game:

Jesus sighs as chips vanish

Alien flips his pistol in frustration

Buddha stays still, eyes closed

Souls scream, chips fly, dice roll
🎥 Quick-cut sequence synced to escalating background music.

📽️ Shot 11: Buddha Wins a Demon (100–110 sec)

Buddha rolls. Wins.
A screaming demon is sucked from hell and rises toward the sky.
Scene shifts to the Pure Land:
Lotus petals float through the air.
Multiple Buddhas meditate midair on floating lotus platforms.
The demon transforms — now at peace.
🎥 Wide shot of serene mountain-and-water landscape with glowing skies.

📽️ Shot 12: Final Fade (110–120 sec)

Craps table fades back in as they laugh, bet again.
Dice roll slowly in close-up.
Voiceover fades in: “Even gods… crave the gamble.”
🎥 Slow fade to black as dice roll in the void.
