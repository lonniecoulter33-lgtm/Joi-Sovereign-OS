Trim size:
6.0″ × 9.0″ (width × height)

Page Margins (Interior)

These margins create calm, balance, and breath.

Top: 1.00″

Bottom: 1.00″

Inner (gutter): 0.90″

Outer: 0.75″

This leaves a live area of approximately:

Width: 4.35″

Height: 7.00″

Plenty of space for floating art and silence.

Floating Art Size (Recommended)

For a contemplative, Zen feel:

Image Dimensions

Width: 4.25″ – 4.50″

Height: auto (preserve aspect ratio)

This equals roughly 70–75% of page width.

Do not stretch the image to fill the live area.

Vertical Placement (Very Important)

Place the image:

Centered horizontally

Slightly above vertical center

Exact Placement Guide

Top of image starts approx 2.25″ – 2.50″ from top of page

Bottom white space should feel slightly heavier than the top

This subtle imbalance feels natural, like a bow.

Page Rules for Image Pages

No page numbers

No headers or footers

No captions

One image per page

Facing page may be blank (optional but powerful)

DPI & Color (Print-Safe)

Resolution: 300 DPI

Color mode: Grayscale or pure black

File format: TIFF or PNG

No bleed required for floating art
